About Ferrero Rocher

Ferrero Rocher is a premium chocolate confection created by the Italian company Ferrero in 1982. It features a whole roasted hazelnut surrounded by a wafer shell filled with hazelnut chocolate, coated in milk chocolate, and adorned with chopped hazelnuts. Packaged in distinctive gold foil wrapping, Ferrero Rocher has become a globally popular luxury treat, often chosen for gifts and special occasions. The company Ferrero, known for its high-quality confections, also produces other well-known products such as Nutella and Kinder.
